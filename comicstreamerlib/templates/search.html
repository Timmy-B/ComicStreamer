Most text fields will accept a "*" as a wildcard. For example:

*Walking*
Brian*Vaughn
Image*


Date fields expect format "YYYY-MM-DD hh:mm:ss". You may omit the any right-most portion. For example:

2014
2014-03
2014-03-12
